Organic Plant Remedies

Natural solutions to protect your plants without harmful chemicals

Effective Organic Treatments

Neem Oil Spray

Effective against aphids, mites, whiteflies and fungal diseases like powdery mildew.

Insecticide Fungicide

Preparation:

  1. Mix 1-2 tbsp cold-pressed neem oil with 1 gallon water
  2. Add 1 tsp mild liquid soap as emulsifier
  3. Shake well before use
  4. Spray on affected plants every 7-14 days

Garlic Pest Spray

Natural repellent for aphids, cabbage worms, and other soft-bodied insects.

Insect Repellent Antiviral

Preparation:

  1. Blend 2 whole garlic bulbs with 1 cup water
  2. Strain and dilute with 1 gallon water
  3. Add 1 tbsp vegetable oil and 1 tsp liquid soap
  4. Spray on plants weekly or after rain

Baking Soda Fungicide

Controls fungal diseases like black spot, powdery mildew, and blight.

Fungicide pH Balancer

Preparation:

  1. Mix 1 tbsp baking soda with 1 gallon water
  2. Add 1 tbsp horticultural oil or liquid soap
  3. Spray on affected plants every 5-7 days
  4. Avoid using in hot weather (>85Â°F)

Chamomile Tea Fungicide

Prevents damping-off disease in seedlings and fights fungal infections.

Fungicide Seedling Care

Preparation:

  1. Steep 2-3 chamomile tea bags in 1 cup boiling water
  2. Let cool and dilute with 1 gallon water
  3. Spray on seedlings or affected plants
  4. Use as soil drench for prevention

Eggshell Pest Barrier

Deters slugs, snails, and cutworms while adding calcium to soil.

Pest Deterrent Soil Amendment

Preparation:

  1. Rinse and dry eggshells thoroughly
  2. Crush into small pieces (not powder)
  3. Sprinkle around plant bases
  4. Reapply after heavy rain

Milk Spray for Viruses

Helps prevent and control mosaic viruses in plants.

Antiviral Fungicide

Preparation:

  1. Mix 1 part milk with 2-3 parts water
  2. Use skim or whole milk (not ultra-pasteurized)
  3. Spray on leaves every 10-14 days
  4. Best applied in morning hours

Cinnamon Antifungal

Prevents fungal growth and deters pests like ants and gnats.

Fungicide Pest Deterrent

Preparation:

  1. Sprinkle ground cinnamon on soil surface
  2. Mix with water for a spray (1 tbsp per cup)
  3. Apply to seedling stems to prevent damping-off
  4. Reapply after watering

Epsom Salt Tonic

Boosts plant growth and prevents magnesium deficiency.

Growth Booster Nutrient Supplement

Preparation:

  1. Dissolve 1 tbsp Epsom salt in 1 gallon water
  2. Use as foliar spray every 2-4 weeks
  3. For soil application: 1 tbsp per foot of plant height
  4. Great for tomatoes and peppers

Banana Peel Fertilizer

Rich in potassium for flowering and fruiting plants.

Fruit Booster Zero Waste

Preparation:

  1. Chop 3-4 banana peels into small pieces
  2. Bury 1-2 inches deep near plant roots
  3. Alternative: Soak peels in water for 3 days to make tea
  4. Apply tea to soil every 2 weeks

Wood Ash Amendment

Raises soil pH and adds potassium/calcium.

Soil Alkalinizer Slug Deterrent

Preparation:

  1. Use only untreated wood ash
  2. Sprinkle thin layer around plants (1/2" max)
  3. Mix into soil for acidic beds
  4. Avoid using with acid-loving plants

Compost Tea

Liquid fertilizer packed with beneficial microbes.

Growth Stimulant Disease Prevention

Preparation:

  1. Place compost in burlap sack (1:5 ratio with water)
  2. Steep in non-chlorinated water for 24-48 hours
  3. Strain and dilute to light tea color
  4. Apply as foliar spray or soil drench weekly

Horsetail Fungicide

High silica content strengthens plants against fungi.

Fungicide Silica Source

Preparation:

  1. Steep 100g fresh horsetail in 1L water for 24 hours
  2. Simmer for 30 minutes and strain
  3. Dilute 1:5 with water before use
  4. Spray every 2 weeks during humid weather
