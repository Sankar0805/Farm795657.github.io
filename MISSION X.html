Crop Virus Encyclopedia

Identify and understand plant pathogens

Virus

Tobacco Mosaic Virus (TMV)

Tomatoes, Tobacco Mechanical transmission

Characteristic mosaic patterns on leaves, stunted growth, and reduced yields. One of the most persistent plant viruses.

Learn Treatment
Virus

Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl (TYLCV)

Tomatoes, Peppers Whitefly transmission

Causes upward curling of leaves, yellowing, flower drop, and severe stunting. Can cause 100% yield loss in tomatoes.

Learn Treatment
Virus

Potatoes viruse Y (PVY)

Potatoes Whitefly transmission

Causes upward curling of leaves, yellowing, flower drop, and severe stunting. Can cause 100% yield loss in tomatoes.
