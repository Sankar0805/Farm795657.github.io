Common Chickpea Diseases and Organic Remedies

Disease

Ascochyta Blight

Symptoms: Dark lesions on stems, pods, and leaves

Organic Remedies:

  • Use disease-free seeds
  • Apply neem oil or garlic extract spray
  • Practice crop rotation
  • Remove infected plant debris
Disease

Pod Borer

Symptoms: Holes in pods, larval feeding on seeds

Organic Remedies:

  • Use pheromone traps
  • Introduce natural enemies like Trichogramma
  • Spray neem seed kernel extract
Disease

Root Rot

Symptoms: Yellowing, stunted growth, decayed roots

Organic Remedies:

  • Use Trichoderma-enriched compost
  • Ensure proper drainage
  • Avoid over-irrigation
  • Solarize soil before planting
