Banana Crop Information

About Banana Cultivation in Andhra Pradesh

Banana is a key fruit crop in coastal Andhra Pradesh. Varieties include Dwarf Cavendish, Robusta, Rasthali, and Poovan.

Soil Requirements

Rich loamy soil with pH 6.5–7.5 and good drainage

Temperature

Optimal temperature is 26–30°C for good growth

Water Needs

Requires regular irrigation; avoid water stagnation

Growing Period

9–12 months depending on variety and care

Common Banana Diseases and Organic Remedies

Fungal

Panama Wilt (Fusarium Wilt)

Symptoms: Yellowing of older leaves, pseudostem splitting, plant wilting

Organic Remedies:

  • Use resistant varieties like G-9
  • Apply Trichoderma enriched compost in planting pits
  • Ensure good field drainage
  • Solarize soil before planting
Bacterial

Moko Disease

Symptoms: Leaf wilting, internal discoloration, fruit blackening

Organic Remedies:

  • Sanitize tools between plants
  • Apply lime + turmeric paste at plant base
  • Remove and destroy infected suckers
  • Plant disease-free tissue culture plants
Viral

Banana Bunchy Top Virus (BBTV)

Symptoms: Bunched leaf appearance at top, stunted growth, dark green streaks on leaf

Organic Remedies:

  • Use virus-free planting material
  • Control aphid vectors using neem oil spray
  • Rogue infected plants immediately
  • Use yellow sticky traps to monitor vectors
