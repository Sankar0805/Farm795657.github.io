Plant Virus Encyclopedia

Comprehensive information about various plant viruses affecting agricultural crops worldwide

Tobacco Mosaic Virus (TMV)

Tobamovirus

Causes mosaic patterns on leaves, stunted growth, and reduced yields in tobacco, tomatoes, and other crops.

Tobacco, Tomato Mechanical transmission

Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus

Begomovirus

Causes upward leaf curling, yellowing, and stunted growth in tomato plants, leading to significant yield loss.

Tomato, Pepper Whitefly transmission

Potato Virus Y (PVY)

Potyvirus

Causes mosaic patterns, leaf necrosis, and tuber necrosis in potatoes, reducing both yield and quality.

Potato, Tomato Aphid transmission

Cucumber Mosaic Virus (CMV)

Cucumovirus

Affects a wide range of plants causing mosaic patterns, leaf distortion, and stunted growth.

Cucumber, Tomato Aphid transmission

Banana Bunchy Top Virus

Babuvirus

Causes stunted growth, bunchy appearance of leaves, and reduced fruit production in banana plants.

Banana Aphid transmission

Citrus Tristeza Virus

Closterovirus

Causes quick decline, stem pitting, and seedling yellows in citrus trees, often leading to tree death.

Orange, Lemon Aphid transmission
