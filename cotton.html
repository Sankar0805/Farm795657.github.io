Cotton Crop Information

About Cotton Cultivation in Andhra Pradesh

Cotton is a major cash crop in Andhra Pradesh, especially in the districts surrounding Visakhapatnam. Popular varieties include Bunny Bt, RCH 2 Bt, and local hybrids.

Common Cotton Diseases and Organic Remedies

Fungal

Alternaria Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Brown circular spots on older leaves with concentric rings

Organic Remedies:

  • Apply neem oil or garlic-chili extract spray
  • Use Trichoderma viride as seed treatment
  • Ensure good field sanitation
  • Avoid overcrowding and excessive nitrogen
Bacterial

Bacterial Blight

Symptoms: Angular black lesions on leaves, blackened veins, and boll rot

Organic Remedies:

  • Use certified disease-free seeds
  • Apply cow dung slurry + turmeric spray (natural bactericide)
  • Intercrop with non-host crops
  • Prune and burn infected plant parts
Viral

Cotton Leaf Curl Virus

Symptoms: Curling of leaves, vein thickening, and stunted growth

Organic Remedies:

  • Spray neem oil or fish oil resin soap
  • Use yellow sticky traps for whitefly control
  • Grow tolerant varieties like RS 2013 or F 1861
  • Maintain field hygiene and weed control
