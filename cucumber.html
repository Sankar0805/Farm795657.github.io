Cucumber Crop Information

About Cucumber Cultivation in Andhra Pradesh

Cucumber is a widely grown vegetable in Andhra Pradesh, especially in coastal districts like Visakhapatnam. It is popular for both fresh consumption and pickling purposes.

Soil Requirements

Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter, pH 6.0-6.8

Temperature

18-30°C (64-86°F) ideal, sensitive to cold

Water Needs

Consistent moisture, avoid both drought and waterlogging

Growing Period

70-90 days from sowing to harvest

Common Cucumber Diseases and Organic Remedies

Fungal

Anthracnose

Symptoms: Sunken spots on fruits and leaves, yellowing and wilting

Organic Remedies:

  • Spray neem oil (5ml/liter) weekly
  • Use Trichoderma viride in soil application
  • Rotate crops regularly
  • Destroy infected plant residues
  • Choose resistant hybrids like Pusa Uday
Bacterial

Bacterial Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Angular brown spots on leaves, greasy appearance

Organic Remedies:

  • Spray copper oxychloride (3g/liter)
  • Use treated seeds
  • Maintain clean and dry fields
  • Avoid splashing irrigation
  • Apply Pseudomonas fluorescens as foliar spray
Viral

Cucumber Mosaic Virus

Symptoms: Mosaic patterns on leaves, leaf distortion, stunted growth

Organic Remedies:

  • Use neem oil to control aphids
  • Plant virus-free seedlings
  • Remove infected plants immediately
  • Use reflective mulches
  • Grow resistant varieties like Poinsett 76
