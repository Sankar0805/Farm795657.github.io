Potato Crop Information

About Potato Cultivation in Andhra Pradesh

Potato is a key vegetable crop in Andhra Pradesh, with Visakhapatnam district seeing growing interest in its cultivation. Cool seasons and well-drained soil support healthy potato growth.

Soil Requirements

Well-drained sandy loam soil, pH 5.0-6.0

Temperature

15-25°C (59-77°F) ideal for tuber formation

Water Needs

Requires moderate watering, avoid waterlogging

Growing Period

3-4 months depending on variety

Common Potato Viruses and Organic Remedies

Virus

Potato Virus Y (PVY)

Symptoms: Mottling, crinkling of leaves, stunted plant growth

Organic Remedies:

  • Apply neem extract weekly to repel aphids
  • Use compost tea to build plant resistance
  • Introduce ladybugs to feed on aphids
  • Remove infected plants immediately
  • Use certified virus-free seed tubers
Virus

Potato Leafroll Virus (PLRV)

Symptoms: Upward rolling leaves, yellowing, poor tuber yield

Organic Remedies:

  • Mulch soil with straw to discourage aphids
  • Introduce parasitic wasps to target pests
  • Apply diluted fish emulsion to enhance plant health
  • Practice crop rotation with legumes
  • Remove volunteer potato plants
Virus

Potato Mop-Top Virus

Symptoms: Leaf distortion, necrotic spots, malformed tubers

Organic Remedies:

  • Enhance drainage to avoid soil-borne spread
  • Solarize soil before planting
  • Apply garlic-chili spray as a natural deterrent
  • Remove symptomatic plants
  • Use mustard as a biofumigant in rotation
